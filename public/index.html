Welcome

Planning a funeral service involves many decisions during a difficult time. This tool is designed to help you select appropriate readings, gospels, music, prayers, and poems for a Christian/Catholic service.

How to Use This Site:

  1. Navigate through the sections (Readings, Gospels, Music, etc.) using the menu above.
  2. Browse the available options. You can preview texts and listen to music samples where available.
  3. Add items you like to your Wishlist by clicking the "Add to Wishlist" button (you'll see these on the content pages). You can select up to two items per category.
  4. Your selected items will appear in the Wishlist section at the bottom of each page.
  5. When you are ready, click the "Generate PDF Plan" button in the Wishlist section to download a document containing your selections and suggested service order.
